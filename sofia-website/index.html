About Me

I'm Sofia Lujan, a 15-year-old content creator from Houston, Texas. I'm homeschooled, which gives me the freedom to pour my energy into what I love most: making content every single day.

I grew up watching my dad build his family-owned construction company from the ground up, and that work ethic rubbed off on me. I train Jiu-Jitsu, and that same discipline I bring to the mat is what drives everything I create.

Content isn't just something I do. It's how I express myself and connect with the world.
